About Our Farm

🏆 Meat Goats – Quality Breeding, Superior Genetics Excellence 🏆

Leading Kenya's meat goat industry with award-winning breeding programs and genetically superior livestock for premium meat production. Our experienced team with over 10 years of expertise ensures exceptional quality from our newly established farm.

The Mountain Goat Farm is a newly established farm in the foothills of Mount Kenya, founded by experienced professionals with over 10 years of expertise in goat breeding and livestock management. Our team's extensive knowledge ensures the highest standards in meat goat production from day one.

We pride ourselves on sustainable farming practices, superior genetics, and quality breeding programs that produce healthy, robust meat goats for both local and international markets.
